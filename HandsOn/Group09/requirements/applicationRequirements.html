Application Requirements Specification: Urban Tree Management Application

Introduction

The proposed application aims to visualize and analyze the state of trees in urban parks in Madrid. The application will allow users to explore the health and condition of trees across various parks, helping the city government and citizens monitor urban greenery.

Functional Requirements

  • FR1: Interactive map showing the location and condition of trees in different parks.
  • FR2: Ability to filter the trees by their condition (e.g., healthy, damaged) and park location.
  • FR3: Detailed view of each tree, including species information, condition, and maintenance history.
  • FR4: Search functionality to find trees based on park name or tree species.

Non-Functional Requirements

  • NFR1: The application must be intuitive and easy to use, with an accessible design for users of all ages.
  • NFR2: The application should perform efficiently, even when handling large datasets, with minimal loading times for maps and data visualizations.
  • NFR3: The data should be updated regularly to reflect real-time changes in tree health and management activities.
