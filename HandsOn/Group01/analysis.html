License Analysis

Publisher and Rightsholder: Ayuntamiento de Madrid.

The dataset from the Madrid City Council is available under a LDA license (Licencia de Datos Abiertos), which allows us to do the following:

  • Download the data for free.
  • Share, modify, adapt, extract and combine the data.
As long as the following conditions are met:
  1. The source must be clearly stated as "Ayuntamiento de Madrid".
  2. It must not be implied or suggested that the Madrid City Council endorses or sponsors the reuse of the data.
  3. Altering or misrepresenting the meaning of the information is prohibited.
  4. Any metadata related to the update date or reuse conditions included in the documents must not be altered or removed.
Additionally, the City Council grants a non-exclusive and free license to use the intellectual property rights needed for the reuse of the data, such as reproduction, distribution, and transformation of the information, in any format, worldwide, and for the maximum period allowed by law. The license we will be using is LDA.
