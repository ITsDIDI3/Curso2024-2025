Dataset Analysis - Air Quality Measurements

1. Data Analysis

The dataset contains air quality measurements from stations in Gijón. It consists of two main entities:

  • Measurement Stations (EstacionMedicion): Fixed locations where measurements are taken
  • Measurements (Medicion): Individual readings of various air quality parameters

Key Data Points

Type Description
Location Data Station number, title, latitude, longitude
Air Quality Parameters SO2, H2S, NO, NO2, NOX, O3
Weather Data Wind direction, wind speed, temperature, humidity

2. Licensing

The original dataset is published by the Gijón City Council and is available through the Spanish Open Data Portal.

The data is released under a Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 Spain License. This is an open license that permits both commercial and non-commercial use of the data, as long as appropriate credit is given.

To maintain consistency and ensure proper data usage, we will apply the same Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 ES license to our derived dataset and ontology. This approach ensures that users can freely use, modify, and share our work while respecting the original data's licensing terms.

3. Resource Naming Strategy

Base URI: http://webcam.linkeddata.Gijon.es.org/

Naming Patterns

  • Stations: estacion_[number] (e.g., estacion_12)
  • Measurements: medicion_[station-number]_[period] (e.g., medicion_12_1)
